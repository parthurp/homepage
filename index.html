Hi! You just landed on Arthur Paté's website!

I'm an associate professor at ISEN in the acoustics team. I act as the deputy director of this team, managing our organization in terms of teaching duties.


To give you to the full picture, ISEN is part of Junia, a consortium grouping three French "engineering schools". I am also with IEMN, a research lab of CNRS.

During the period 2021-2022, I serve as the chair of the group SRGNO at SFA (Section Grand Nord de la Société Française d'Acoustique / Group of acousticians in the North of France, French Acoustical Society)
